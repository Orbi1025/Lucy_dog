About

The creation of $Lucy is inspired by a heartwarming story: Vitalik Buterin's family adopted a dog named Lucy from an animal shelter in Ukraine. This token has no intrinsic value or expected financial return.

It stands as a beautiful symbol of compassion, hope, and the connection between the crypto world and humanitarian care. Lucy's journey reminds us that even small acts of kindness can bring immense warmth.
